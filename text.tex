\section{Spell Checking}
\cmd{aspell -t -c file.tex} , t option is for \TeX{} or \LaTeX{} format.\\
\cmd{aspell -t -c file.tex \&\& pdflatex file.tex} checking and compiling at the same time.\\
\cmd{cat file.tex | aspell list -t | sort |uniq} get a list of misspelled words non-interactively.
